Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

Kale and quinoa provide a healthy base for roasted chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time:45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 chicken breast
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 in middle. Time: 40 minutes
  2. Toss quinoa, chicken, kale, and Caesar dressing until coated. Time: 4 minutes
  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish. Time: 1 minute
